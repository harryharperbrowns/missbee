WELCOME TO OUR CAMPAIGN

Bees in the UK are in serious decline. It’s time to act. Get involved NS create a buzz. The UK’s bee population has fallen by 30% and wild honey bees are nearly extinct in many parts of the UK Bees pollinate the plants that we grow for food. Bees pollinate the wild plants that provide food for much of the UK’s wildlife. If the bees disappear – so will a third of our food Bees have lost over 90% of their wildflower meadows – their food and shelter is disappearing - and so are the bees Remember: Halt the decline of the bees. Back the missbee campaign.
